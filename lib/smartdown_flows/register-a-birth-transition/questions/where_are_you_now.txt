# Where are you now?

[choice: where_are_you_now]
* same_country: In the country where the child was born
* another_country: In another country
* in_the_uk: In the UK

* where_are_you_now is 'same_country'
  * oru_exception(child_country) => outcome_embassy_in_the_uk_or_same_country
  * oru_country(child_country) => outcome_oru_in_the_uk_or_same_country
  * otherwise => outcome_embassy_in_the_uk_or_same_country
* where_are_you_now is 'in_the_uk' => outcome_oru_in_the_uk_or_same_country
* where_are_you_now is 'another_country' => registration_country
